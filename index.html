nyamachoma (also known as nyamachoma world or nyamachomaworld) is the photography and filmmaking project of nyamachoma. The work focuses on film photography, portrait photography, studio photography, and visual storytelling. nyamachoma photography features 35mm film photography shot on Kodak Gold 200, Cinestill 800T, Kodak Portra 400, Kodak Portra 800, and Ilford HP5 Plus. nyamachoma film work also includes visuals shot on MiniDV tape, exploring cinematic portraiture, studio lighting, and film-based photography techniques. nyamachoma film photography explores portrait photography, studio photography, and cinematic lighting techniques.
